## What is 3D visualization

3D visualization is a photorealistic image of a future interior created on a computer. It allows you to "see" the result before renovation work begins.

### Why visualization is needed before renovation

**1. Budget savings**
- Visualization shows exact materials and furniture
- You can change the design before purchasing expensive items
- Eliminates errors in layout and proportions

**2. Coordination with contractors**
- Builders see the exact result
- Fewer questions and misunderstandings on site
- All decisions are visually documented

**3. Confidence in the result**
- You know exactly how your home will look
- You can compare several design options
- Emotionally "live" in the space before it's created

### Work stages
