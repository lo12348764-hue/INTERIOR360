## Main trends of 2025

Interior design in 2025 is a balance between minimalism and warmth, technology and nature.

### 1. Organic forms

Soft, rounded lines replace sharp corners:
- Furniture with smooth contours
- Arched doorways and niches
- Rounded mirrors and lamps

### 2. Warm neutral palettes

Cream, beige, warm gray — the basis for cozy interiors:
- Layered textures in one palette
- Natural wood accents
- Soft contrast through lighting

### 3. Natural materials

Wood, stone, ceramics — priority over synthetics:
- Large format ceramic tiles
- Solid wood furniture
- Decorative plaster

### 4. Hidden storage

Minimalism requires order:
- Built-in furniture from floor to ceiling
- Hidden doors and storage systems
- Handle-free facades

### 5. Lighting as decor

Light fixtures are not just function, but art objects:
- Pendant compositions
- Hidden lighting
- Accent spotlights

### 6. Greenery in interior

Plants are an essential element:
- Greenery in bathrooms and kitchens
- Vertical gardens
- Large floor plants
