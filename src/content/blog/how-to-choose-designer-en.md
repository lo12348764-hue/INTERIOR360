## Why choosing the right designer matters

Choosing an interior designer is a decision that lasts for years. The professional's expertise determines whether you get a comfortable, beautiful and functional space or disappointment with endless revisions.

### Evaluation criteria

**1. Portfolio and style**
- Does the designer's style match your preferences
- Is there a variety of projects (residential, commercial)
- Quality of visualizations and level of detail

**2. Work process**
- How the initial consultation goes
- Is there a contract and work stages
- How revisions and corrections are handled

**3. Budget and timeline**
- Pricing transparency
- Realistic completion timelines
- Whether author supervision is included

### Questions to ask

1. How many projects have you completed in the last year?
2. Can I see reviews from your clients?
3. How do you handle changes during the process?
4. Do you provide drawings for builders?

### Red flags

- No contract or it's too general
- Won't show portfolio or all projects look similar
- Unreasonable timelines without explanation
- Don't listen to your wishes at the first meeting
